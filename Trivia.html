General

Name

The name "Gaara" literally means either "self-loving demon" or "self-loving silk" (我愛羅).

Popularity

In the Naruto character popularity poll, Gaara is one of four characters to have always placed in the top ten of every poll.

Shukaku's previous jinchūriki foretold that one day someone would show it the meaning of the words which were tattooed on his own hands: "accept" (受, ukeru) and "heart" (心, kokoro).[45] The tattoo on Gaara's forehead, "love" (愛, ai), is a combination of these two kanji.
